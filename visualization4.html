The West and East coasts of the continental US have a reputation of having different style. Tempo is just one element of a song that can be quantifiably compared. The graph clearly shows the mountain time zone having sparse music production. Two longitudes that had a broad range of tempos as well as high number of songs are -118.24532 and -74.00712, which are Los Angeles and New York City reflectively. Most song appear to be produced in the Central and East time zones, evenly spread among tempos. Another longitude to inquire about would be around -90 degrees.
